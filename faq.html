Getting Started

What is FlowRead? +

FlowRead is a speed reading app that helps you read faster and absorb more information in less time. The app uses the scientifically proven RSVP method (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation).

Benefits:

  • Increase reading speed by up to 200%
  • Import texts from the web, PDF or EPUB
  • Personalized training with progress tracking
  • Offline mode - read without internet
How does Speed Reading work? +

Speed Reading is based on the RSVP method (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation). Instead of your eyes moving across the text, words are displayed one at a time at a fixed point.

Key principles:

  1. Fixation Point: Each word is displayed at the same position, so your eyes don't need to move.
  2. ORP Marking: The "Optimal Recognition Point" of each word is highlighted.
  3. Subvocalization Reduction: The increased speed reduces internal vocalization.
  4. Progressive Increase: You start at your current reading speed and gradually improve.
What reading speed is normal? +

Average reading speeds:

  • 200-250 WPM: Average reader
  • 300-400 WPM: Good reader
  • 400-600 WPM: Fast reader
  • 600+ WPM: Speed reader

With FlowRead you can gradually increase your speed from 300 to 1200 WPM.

App Usage

How do I import texts? +

There are several ways:

  1. Web Browser: Open the built-in browser, navigate to an article and tap "Read"
  2. Paste Text: Copy text and paste it via "Paste Text"
  3. Files: Import EPUB or PDF files from your Files app
  4. Share Extension: Share articles directly from Safari to FlowRead
How do I change the speed? +

While reading:

  • Tap the display to show controls
  • Use the +/- buttons to adjust WPM
  • Swipe up/down for quick adjustments

In settings:

  • Open settings via the gear icon
  • Under "Reading Speed" you can set your default WPM
What is "Adaptive Mode"? +

Adaptive mode automatically adjusts the display time based on each word's complexity:

  • Short words: Displayed faster
  • Long words: Get more time
  • Punctuation: Natural pauses at commas and periods
  • Common words: Are recognized faster

The result is a more natural reading flow while maintaining high speed.

Can I read offline? +

Yes! All saved articles are available offline.

  • Imported texts are stored locally on your device
  • Training sessions work without internet
  • Statistics sync when you're back online
